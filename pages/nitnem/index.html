Nitnem: The Daily Prayers

Begin and end your day with the divine words of the Gurus. Nitnem (Daily Discipline) is a collection of sacred hymns to be read daily, providing spiritual strength and guidance.

The foundational prayer that explains the nature of God and the path to spiritual union.
